The process

How Capsule Club
actually works.

From your first quiz question to a complete capsule in your inbox — here's exactly what to expect.

01
The quiz

Tell us about your wardrobe — and your life.

Our style quiz takes about 8 minutes. You'll tell us your five brands, your capsule zone (Everyday, Work, or Weekend & Social), your budget per piece, your sizing notes, and what your biggest wardrobe problem actually is.

We also ask about what you already own and love — because the best capsule works with your existing wardrobe, not against it. If you already have great denim, we skip that slot. If your bag situation is solved, we don't sell you another one.

"A capsule for someone working from home looks completely different from one for someone in an office five days a week. We need to understand your actual life before we recommend anything."
02
The curation

We build your capsule from your exact brands.

Every piece we recommend comes from your selected brands. We don't suggest a better brand. We don't substitute one you didn't choose. If your brands can't strongly fill a slot, we tell you honestly and explain why.

Each capsule slot gets four options: Option A (our top pick), Option B (a same-brand alternative), Option C (a different brand from your list), and Option D (a budget alternative). You always have a path forward, whatever happens with stock.

"We name the specific product — not 'a white tee from Gap' but 'the VintageSoft Tee in White, size S.' Vague recommendations are useless. We name names."
03
The delivery

A beautifully formatted capsule in your inbox.

Your capsule arrives as a formatted PDF within 48 hours of completing your quiz. Each slot is named (not numbered), has all four options with direct brand links, notes on availability, a free shipping strategy for each brand, and sizing guidance specific to your measurements.

Club+ members receive their capsule within 24 hours and can request revisions until they're happy with every slot.

"The capsule PDF ends with one line: 'Your Everyday capsule is complete. When you're ready for your Work capsule, your preferences are already saved.' That's the whole business model in one sentence."
04
The progression

Your next capsule starts where this one ended.

When you're ready for your next zone, you don't start from scratch. Your brands, your budget, your sizing notes — all saved. We ask only what might have changed: new season, new brands, different wardrobe gap.

Three zones. Three capsules. One complete wardrobe built over time, at a pace that suits your life and your budget.

"We deliberately don't offer a 'Full Rotation' upfront. A single zone first — low commitment, immediate value. Then we earn the next zone by delivering on the first."

Common questions

Do I pay again for my next zone? +
Yes — each zone is a separate capsule and priced individually, unless you purchased a Bundle or White Glove package upfront. Your preferences, brands, and sizing are all saved so the next zone takes less time to build. Many members find it useful to space zones out by a few months rather than doing everything at once.
What if I want to update my capsule for a new season? +
A seasonal refresh is a new capsule order — same zone, same brands, updated for what's available and seasonally appropriate right now. We treat it like a new capsule but it's faster to build because your preferences are already saved. Priced the same as a new single capsule.
What's included in a revision? +
A revision means you can swap out any slot you're not happy with, change the budget on a slot, update your brand list, or ask us to rethink a specific recommendation. We rebuild only the slots that need changing and re-deliver within 24 hours.
Do I have to spend money on clothes to use Capsule Club? +
No. Your capsule is a recommendation, not an order. You buy what makes sense, when it makes sense. We flag what you already own so you're never pressured to replace something that's working.

Our story

A cart I couldn't
bring myself to check out.

Capsule Club started with a $802.80 Quince cart and a question I couldn't answer: why does the same amount of money feel completely different at one store versus five?

I've been trying to build a capsule wardrobe for years. Last month I finally figured out why I kept failing — and it had nothing to do with discipline or taste.

It started with Quince. Clean basics, luxury-adjacent fabrics, prices that feel almost suspiciously reasonable. My coworkers were wearing it. My algorithm was full of it. I genuinely liked the aesthetic. So one evening I opened the site with real intention.

Thirteen items. $802.80. Free shipping, at least. I looked at that number for a long time. And I closed the tab.

"$800 at one brand felt like a catalogue. The same $800 across four brands felt like a wardrobe."
Stephanie · Founder

That distinction sounds small. It isn't. It's why I'd been abandoning carts for years without ever understanding why. Every capsule wardrobe guide told me to commit to one brand — "shop your whole wardrobe from Everlane," "build it all from COS." Clean, simple, coherent. The problem is that nobody actually shops that way.

We have our Madewell jeans and our Uniqlo basics and that one COS blazer we saved up for and the Quince cashmere we impulse-bought. We're already multi-brand people. The advice was asking us to be someone we're not.

What I actually needed was someone to take the brands I already love and show me how to build something that works across all of them. A capsule from my brands, not their brands. That's why I built Capsule Club.

About the service

What Capsule Club is and how it works.
What exactly is Capsule Club? +
Capsule Club is a wardrobe curation service that builds personalised capsule wardrobes from the brands you already love. You tell us up to five brands, we build your capsule — piece by piece, with specific product names, outfit combinations, sizing guidance, and direct links to buy. You choose your zone (Everyday, Work, or Weekend & Social) and we curate around that.
How is this different from a personal stylist? +
Personal stylists typically introduce you to new brands and curate from the whole market. We do the opposite — we work exclusively from the brands you already chose. We also document everything: specific products, sizing notes, alternatives, free shipping strategies. A stylist gives you outfits; we give you a system.
How long does it take to receive my capsule? +
The Club members receive their capsule within 48 hours of completing the style quiz. Club+ members receive theirs within 24 hours. If we have any questions about your quiz answers, we'll reach out before building rather than guess.
What if my brands don't have great options for a slot? +
We tell you honestly. If a slot can't be strongly filled from your selected brands, we'll note it as a gap, explain why, and recommend the best available option with a flag that it's not the ideal pick. We never pad a capsule with mediocre recommendations.
Can I request changes to my capsule? +
Club members can request one round of revisions per capsule. Club+ members have unlimited revisions until they're happy with every slot. Just reply to the delivery email with your feedback.

The brands

Questions about the brand database.
What brands do you support? +
We currently support 21 brands including Madewell, Uniqlo, Banana Republic, Gap, Quince, Everlane, COS, Zara, Mango, Aritzia, Reformation, Sézane, J.Crew, Abercrombie & Fitch, Anthropologie, LOFT, Universal Standard, Cuyana, and the factory lines for Gap, Banana Republic, and J.Crew. We add new brands regularly — see The Brands page for the full list.
What if my favourite brand isn't on the list? +
Tell us in your quiz under "anything else we should know." If a brand appears in enough member requests, we'll research and add it. We'd rather add the right brands well than rush to cover everything.
Do you have a bias toward certain brands? +
We earn affiliate commissions on some brands and not others. Our rule (Rule 11 in our curation logic): when two options are genuinely equal, we prefer the affiliate brand. We never recommend an inferior piece to earn a commission. Non-affiliate brands like Quince and Sézane appear in capsules whenever they're the right choice — period.

Sizing and fit

How we handle sizing across different brands.
How do you handle sizing differences between brands? +
Every brand in our database has been researched for sizing quirks — Uniqlo runs small, COS runs large, Sézane uses French sizing, Reformation runs small in the bust. Your capsule includes specific sizing notes per brand, not just a generic size. We tell you exactly what to order.
Do you support petite, tall, and plus sizes? +
Yes. Our quiz includes dedicated fit questions covering silhouette preferences, proportion notes, and size range. Every brand entry in our database includes a sizing matrix across petite, tall, plus, and curvy. We'll flag brands that don't serve your size range well and suggest alternatives from your list.

Billing and membership

Subscriptions, pausing, and everything money-related.
Can I pause instead of cancel? +
Yes — always. You can pause your membership for up to three months and resume whenever you're ready. Your preferences, brands, and saved capsules are preserved during a pause. We'd rather you pause than cancel and lose everything we've built together.
Do I have to spend money on clothes to use Capsule Club? +
No. Your capsule is a recommendation, not an order. We note what you already own so we never pressure you to replace something that's working. Buy what makes sense when it makes sense.
What's the affiliate disclosure? +
Some brands in our database have affiliate programmes that pay us a small commission when you buy through our links. This is one of two revenue streams alongside your subscription fee. The commission comes from the brand, never from you — the price you pay is always the same as going directly to the brand. Your capsule is never influenced by which brands pay us.
